The Best animes according to Prashareet

My top 5 animes of all time are:

The following animes are not in any specific order, everyone of them is great!

Death note

Great psycological thriller anime, every episode gave me goosebumps!
A great anime to begin with if you are new to anime!

Naruto

It has everything, you will feel connected to all the characters.
Although it is a big series anime, but every epsiode is fun!

Demon Slayer

One of my favourite animes of all time. Great animation,story and power system.
You cant help but love it!

Jujutsu Kaisen

"Throughout heaven and hell, I alone am the honoured one"- Gojo Satoru

Your lie in April

A romantic anime, best if you watch it with your partner and cry together.
